Question: How do I get the PlayStation documentation?

You have to become an official developer for the PlayStation. Contact you local SCE office for more details. Becoming a developer requires you to pay a licence fee and signing a non-disclosure agreement. If you are an unemployed student you have probably no chance in fulfilling these requirements.
