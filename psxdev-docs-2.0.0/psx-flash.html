  • DESCRIPTION OF psx-flash
    
psx-flash flashes/updates the firmware EEPROM of the ActionReplay/GameBuster/whatsoever cartridge. If your cartridge EEPROM is broken and you are in trouble, you can use the emergeny mode reflash. You should contact dbalster@psxdev.de for further informations. If you prefer me to do your emergency reflash you should contact me also, but I have to charge some money for that service since it is a bit of work.
